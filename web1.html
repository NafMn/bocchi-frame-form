Bocchi The Rock

Bocchi the Rock! is a band comedy 4-koma manga series by Aki Hamazi for story and illustration. The manga series serialized in Manga Time Kirara MAX by Houbunsha since February 2018 issue and collected four volumes in tank≈çbon format. It become a popular series for its successor of K-ON! and ranked 8th place in the Next Manga Awards 2019. An anime series produced by CloverWorks and premiered on October 9, 2022. In this story, Hitori Gotoh is a high school girl who's starting to learn to play the guitar because she dreams of being in a band, but she's so shy that she hasn't made a single friend. However, her dream might come true after she meets Nijika Ijichi, a girl who plays drums and is looking for a new guitarist for her band.
